About Our Club

We are a student-run Aeronautical Club focused on building RC aircraft, drones, and CubeSats. We run workshops, contests, and project teams — perfect for juniors looking to learn and contribute.

What we build

  • RC planes & control systems
  • Flight dynamics & aerodynamic testing
  • CubeSat payload development and telemetry
  • Drone payload and autonomous navigation

Opportunities

Hands-on roles, mentorship, competition teams, research assistants. We welcome all skill levels — learn with peers and mentors.
